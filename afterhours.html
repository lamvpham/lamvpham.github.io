Let me reintroduce

myself for the first time.

As I’m starting my career in the design & tech industry, I noticed how it bled into my personal life. I have product & graphic design roles in clubs/organizations, I went to design-oriented events; everything was for my design skills and learnings to grow as I continue forward with my career.

But when someone asked me about my hobbies or life outside of work, I noticed that there wasn’t too much I could list out. I didn’t want my profession to be my personality.

Beyond user research or information architectures, I process the world through an artistic & creative lense. This mindset I have captures a range of my thoughts and feelings, from ecstasy and melancholy, to longing and nostalgia, and it becomes an artifact of different forms that I can use to communicate my self through.

Through this, it will answer: who am I outside of design & tech?

Podcast

“This one’s for you” is a podcast about young adults navigating through life, careers in their industry, and not knowing what is going on in life.

From the perspective of myself, Raewyn Tsai, and occasional guests, we gather on a call to have a reflective conversation about our experiences through life, its impact on who we are today, and who we want to become in the future.
