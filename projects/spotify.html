Discover Together

A platform to analyze two people's Spotify music tastes, then curate a playlist made specifically for them.

Context

Overview

Over the course of 5 weeks, my team and I designed and developed a user-facing website to discover new music with the people close to oneself. We believe that sharing music with others brings people closer, and by combining people's music taste with their friends, they both can discover new music, together.

Role

Working with a team of six, my responsibilites entailed designing an accessible and aesthetic design that can correspond on a mobile device, as well as desktop. I focused on the task flow between pages, making sure the front-end matched with the design, and creating a unique aesthetic that's visually pleasing and welcoming to new and returning users.

Being a part of this team taught me a more in-depth experience of building a project into a wide-spread platform, helping me understand from a more holistic perspective how each role can impact something greater.

Introduction

In the beginning

When I joined the Discover Together team, the project was already conceptualized by Arjun and Trustin, where the backend capabilities were functional. The website lacked the visual appeal, which is where they brought on myself and Ann Li to revamp the design.

The page flow

The flow of pages for this originally consisted of a new user going onto the website and the first page is to authorize their Spotify account. After that, it leads to the second page, where it shares the user their Discover Together unique link and their top 50 songs.

For the second user who clicked the linked shared by the first user, it leads to the Spotify authorization page to access their music data. From there, it goes to the Spotify platform with the playlist loaded up.

Prototype

Designing the aesthetic

The main task was creating a welcoming and friendly UI that was easy for development and kept a high rentention rate with users. To do that, I created three different landing page designs based off of minimalist styles, keeping in mind the frontend capabilities.

After designing these options, I discussed with the team the aesthetic direction that they want this platform to go in. The second option was unanimously chosen by the team for its soft ambient lights theme, where it has a welcoming atmosphere for new and returning users.

Keeping in mind the accessibility of the platform, the design has clear text sections and components are easily readable with high contrast between foreground and background.

Branding

Representing the platform

For the platform to have a distinct brand identity, we needed a logo that people can recognize while communicating the platform's aesthetic direction.

The first logo idea is a play of ocean waves and frequency waves, where both are overlapping each other to show 'two music tastes intersecting'. The second idea resembles over-head headphones being shared between two people.
