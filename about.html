Hey Lam,

Why do you design?

because we all see the world differently, why not make it easier for everyone to understand?


From the age of 8, the first thing I ever drew was from a Pokémon card. The shiny colours and unique creatures fascinated me and I replay those memories from time to time. Those moments broadened my creative mind, making me view the small things in life to be more memorable. As life went by, I was able to grasp the more abstract things in life and give them more purpose and meaning to my reality.

Being in Systems Design Engineering, it definitely expanded my idea of what engineering could be and how I can be a part of that community. My deep interest in the arts enabled me to step in the field of design, where I can showcase my abilities while taking in all the new experiences that the future has in store.

Moving forward, I want to be able to branch out and use design to connect concepts, cultural moments, and people in significant and magical ways that everyone can understand. Using the motivation gained from my life experiences, it will be a lofty goal, but I want to inspire people the way that those Pokemon cards did for me.

Outside of work, catch me exploring the local food and cafe scene, playing Super Smash Bros, playing music, drawing art, and bouncing new ideas with friends for future projects.

I hope you enjoyed what you see here and would love to chat more over some coffee! If you like what you see, here's my resume :)
